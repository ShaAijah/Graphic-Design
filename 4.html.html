How to make a Bracelet
1 Choose several strands of embordery thread in a variety of colors.
2 Measure and cut your first strand
3 Use the first strand to measure the remaining strands
4 Tape 8 strands together at the top so it doesnt move while you work
5 Pass the first strand over the second strand to tie a knot
6 Repeat the same knot using the same strands
